Our Mission

Pinky's Kitchen provides not only the finest delicacies, but we also give back to our community in many ways.

Pinky's Kitchen strives to be the most sustainable restaurant in Mew York by sourcing our ingredients locally, supplementing produce with herbs grown on our rooftop garden, and giving back to the community through urban farming education.
